Everything you need to know about Instant Tea

Instant tea is a type of tea powder and similar to instant coffee crystal granules, which can be easily dissolved in water to make tea from it. It is very easy to use and also very tasty.

Tea(source: Pixabay)

How is Instant Tea made?

Instant tea is made using either whole, processed leaves, fresh tea leaves, or undried fermented leaves. Liquids are extracted from the leaves and turned into a concentrate. This concentrate is then dried and turned into a powder through either spray drying, freeze drying, or the vacuum drying process.

Health benefits

Instant tea health benefits are very similar to tea bags one. It contains plenty antioxidants like flavonoids and catechins which can protect the body from pollution.

It also reduces risk of heart attack and stroke by lowering LDL cholesterol ("bad" cholesterol) and prevents tooth decay.

Drinking tea also keeps you hydrated and can either give you energy for the day or make you feel more relaxed at the end of the day.
